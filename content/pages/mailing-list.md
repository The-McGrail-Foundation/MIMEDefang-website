## About MIMEDefang

This list is intended for users of the MIMEDefang e-mail scanner. It is meant for discussion of problems, ideas, and enhancements.
To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the [MIMEDefang Archives](https://marc.info/?l=mimedefang). 

---

## Using MIMEDefang

To post a message to all the list members, send email to mimedefang@lists.mimedefang.org.
You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription by following those [instructions](https://lists.mimedefang.org/mailman/listinfo/mimedefang_lists.mimedefang.org).
